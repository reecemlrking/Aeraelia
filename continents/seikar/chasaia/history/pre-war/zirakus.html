The city of Zirakus was an ancient magocratic city from the first age. Little is known about it today outside of a few remnants which survived the Sunderings. What Arlax has pieced together from various scholars' work is the following:
  • Zirakus was founded by the Zirakus family, an ancient family of mages of which little is known.
  • The city existed for centuries prior to the first war, but was destroyed/disappeared
  • Much of the details of its destruction are lost to time.
  • Very few remnants of the city have been discovered.
